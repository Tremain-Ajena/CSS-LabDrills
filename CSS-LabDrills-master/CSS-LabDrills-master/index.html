Dolls

Dolls is a 1980's horror/b-movie that focuses on a young girl, her dysfunctional family, and a group of young travelers that seek refuge at house in the middle of nowhere during a storm that appears seemingly out of nowhere. The movie at heart is about learning to keep your innocence and kindness intact even as you mature into an adult.

Candyman

Candyman is a movie that does not really need an introduction. The movie centers around the mythical figure Candyman, played by Tony Todd, and how he thrives off of the rumors that the people of his "congregation" whisper about him. He presents himself more within our world as his existence is threatened by a doctoral thesis candidate named Helen who seeks to explore how myths are created by the oppressed as a form of escapism. This movie is a truly interesting tale with a lot to analyze and digest.

Rose Red

This mini-series focuses on a group of clairvoyent individuals that volunteer to stay in a mansion that has been said to be haunted and continues to expand in size despite being vacant for decades. As the story progresses bloodlines and secrets are revealed and the group of people struggle to stay sane and hold onto their lives as they are picked off one by one to feed the hunger of the house.
